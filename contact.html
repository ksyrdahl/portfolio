Hi I'm Kaelyn Syrdahl, a forward-thinking UI/UX designer with a passion for creating solutions that resonate globally. I recently earned my BA in Design from UC Davis, with a focus on UI/UX and graphic design. My design philosophy is simple: while Art "inspires" people, Design "creates solutions." In my world, design is more than aesthetics; it's a powerful tool for problem-solving. I find inspiration in the idea that every design has the potential to be a solution, addressing real-world issues and enhancing the way people interact with the world.

My heart lies in UI/UX design because of its unparalleled ability to reach people. I am captivated by the notion that the interfaces we create have the potential to not only simplify tasks but also evoke emotions and leave lasting impressions. Design, to me, is the bridge that connects innovation with human experience.

Having spent over 11 years immersed in a dual immersion school, I developed a profound appreciation for language and a deep understanding of diverse cultures. Growing up surrounded by different perspectives taught me to be open-minded and see life from different points of view. This cultural richness profoundly influences my design approach, fostering inclusivity and cultural sensitivity in every project.

Fluent in Spanish and on a continuous journey with Japanese, my multilingual background enhances my ability to communicate effectively and empathize with users from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds.
